Calibration The model has four structural parameters. Determine yours from reflective questions, or set them directly.

γ — Life-Balance Aversion
Would you rather have all three dimensions of life (income, health, meaning) at 6/10 — or one at 9 and another at 3? 1 = happy to sacrifice one for another · 10 = need them all balanced
5
If your health declined sharply but your income stayed high, how much would that bother you relative to the reverse? 1 = money compensates for most things · 10 = no amount of money offsets poor health
6
η — Weakest-Link Sensitivity
Can someone with a serious chronic condition still lead a deeply fulfilling life? 1 = absolutely, other dimensions compensate fully · 10 = a collapsed dimension ruins everything
4
If you were financially comfortable but had no sense of purpose or connection, how liveable is that? 1 = fine, comfort is enough · 10 = meaninglessness is intolerable regardless of wealth
6
δ — Leisure Dividend from Retirement
How much does your work currently drain versus energise you? 1 = I love my work, retirement would bore me · 10 = work is a grind, I live for time off
5
Do you have activities, relationships, or projects that would expand meaningfully if you had more free time? 1 = not really · 10 = extensive plans and passions waiting
6
How optimistic are you about your physical capacity to enjoy leisure in your 60s and 70s? 1 = I expect significant limitations · 10 = I expect to be active and capable
6
ρ — Time Preference (Patience)
Would you rather have £10,000 now or £13,000 in three years? 1 = take the money now, always · 10 = I'd wait for the larger sum without hesitation
5
How confident are you that institutions (NHS, state pension, social safety net) will function well when you're old? 1 = very worried — better to consume value now · 10 = confident the future is well-provisioned
4
If everyone retired at 55, would the economy collapse or would automation and productivity absorb it? 1 = total collapse, dependency crisis · 10 = society would adapt fine, work is already over-fetishised
4
