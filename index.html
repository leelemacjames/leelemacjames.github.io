Blusterer of issues in and around physics and mathematics education.

I teach IB Physics and Mathematics at Dwight School London, and I build interactive tools to make the ideas tactile. 250+ apps deployed across GitHub Pages and Vercel, spanning everything from quantum error correction to the Stern-Brocot tree.

Outside the classroom I work on the connections between Eisenstein arithmetic and dark matter confinement, chiral gravity and the reality conditions of GL(2,ℂ) Einstein-Maxwell theory, and the surprisingly deep relationship between Farey sequences and Lorentz transformations.

McMurmerings is where the two meet: the research that informs the teaching, and the teaching that makes the research honest.
