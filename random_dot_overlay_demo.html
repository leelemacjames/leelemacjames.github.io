Random dot overlay

Two random-dot fields are drawn on the same canvas. Field B can be a copy of field A or an independent field. Rotation, scaling, and translation can be controlled separately.

Angular/scale transformation and translation are controlled separately. The strongest visual effects usually appear when field B is a copy of field A.
